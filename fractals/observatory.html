Detalhes e Mecânicas

Visualizando: Mecânicas Normais

Boss 1 - Skorvald

Avoid cone kicks and eruptions. Position character to look away when boss has an Eye above it's head. 66%/33% glide into rifts to kill flux anomalies around edges

Ball Puzzle

Entering the next area you have Hypernova Launch special action key to dash quickly; enter the left portal, then traverse the islands killing enemies, one island requires using Hypernova Launch to juggle balls to progress

Boss 2 - Artsariiv

Use Hypernova Launch to land on her platform. Kill her clones at 66%/33%, dodge or Hypernova Launch to dodge shockwaves. 66% must juggle balls like you did on the islands. Red skull over head (Corporeal Reassignment) means run into the center bubble

Boss 3 - Arkk

Push Solar Blooms clockwise into reactors at 100/80/50/30% using attacks, use Nova Launch special action key to avoid projectiles. When boss has an orb over head prepare to use Hypernova Launch to dodge shockwave after it disappears. When boss has an eye over head, position your character to look away. In T4 only at 40% health: platforms vanish - glide to sides, kill anomalies and regroup

[CM] Skorvald

More frequently casts normal mode attacks, starts with a breakbar. If bomb over your head, leave group and dodge final explosion. Dodge charging attacks at 66%. Enrage at 33% with line eruption knockbacks - stay close and dodge, stability is clutch

[CM] Artsariiv

Now has more AoE size on most attacks and clones spawned, and the Eye mechanic is added. Must kill Temporal Anomaly quickly to spawn bubble in middle

[CM] Arkk

Starts with Eye mechanic, must still kill Temporal Anomaly to get the bubble to protect from skull. 70% an add spawns and Arkk teleports to outer area to cast Eye attack, look away. 40% platforms begin to fall then get launched, if you do use Hypernova Launch to land back on platform
