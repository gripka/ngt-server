Detalhes e Mecânicas

Visualizando: Mecânicas Normais

Traversal

Use Survey Gear to grapple and glide between platforms

Mini-boss - Dolus

Turn away when an eye appears over his head (Dread Visage) and spread for when roman numeral is over player heads (Frightening Speed)

Boss - Kanaxai

Look away from eyes, drop axes on the edge and spread numbered marks

Break bar every 10%

Break bar every 10% to prevent aspect spawns, then grapple to visible aspects to kill them

At 66/33%

Use Survey Gear to kill tethered aspects quickly before World Cleaver AoE casts - if out of gear charges collect blue orbs from around the main arena to recharge

[CM] Mini-boss - Dolus

Now immediately casts Dread Visage, don't be looking when he spawns

[CM] Boss - Kanaxai

From 66% and lower, he will teleport to a random island during Dread Visage you must not be looking at. Easy strat is to just look east since there is no island

[CM] 33% and lower

He will cast Dread Visage during roman numeral attack, be sure you are looking away during it
