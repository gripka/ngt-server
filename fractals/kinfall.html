Detalhes e Mecânicas

Visualizando: Mecânicas Normais

Exploration

Some doors require players give up their Life Fire (circle that protects from the environment) to open, stay together to protect players without it

Adds Management

Adds are simple throughout, focus down smaller adds first then big ones if available

Final Boss - Frost Tyrant

Two random players have Life Fire, stick together to share with group. Break defiance bar quickly at 75/50/25% or someone's Life Fire will be snuffed. Avoid Freezing Vortex swirls, arrows coming from boss, half room AoEs, and run out if you get a target marker over your head

Cryoflash Mechanic

Boss spins and small red circles appear around the room (Cryoflash): If the circle has an up arrow above it - go inside it, if the small circle has an X, don't stand in them. These alternate every cast

[CM] Resource Stealers

Resource stealers appear throughout the fractal: CC Frost Wisps to restore Life-Fire they've taken

[CM] Final Boss Enhancements

Cryoflash now instantly kills players that fail it

[CM] Wintry Orb

Green circle (Wintry Orb): stack on green player to share damage safely

[CM] Hailstorm

Hailstorm: player targeted ground AoEs - spread briefly to avoid overlaps
