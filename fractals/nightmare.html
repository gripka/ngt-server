Boss 1 - MAMA

CC Break Bar Stack na Safe Zone Dodge Orbs

Break bar to stop spin, dodge orbs, step out of poison, stack in safe zone for the nuke

Interlude

Coordenação Priorize Outer Circles Evite Red Orbs

Capture circles while avoiding red orbs, collect blue orbs for healing and cleansing, capture outer circles and kill adds before inner

Boss 2 - Siax

CC Rápido ou Wipe Cones Para Fora Poison Jogador Distante

Break bar quickly to avoid nuke, point orange cones away from team, poison puddle drops on furthest player - keep them away from team

Boss 3 - Ensolyss

CC ou Wipe Atrás do Boss Stack na Bubble 66/33% Circles

Break bar to avoid nuke, stand behind boss and avoid room wide charges and cones. If bubble forms, stand in it to avoid nuke. 66/33% capture circles, dodging red orbs and jumping shockwaves - all downed players resurrect once points are captured. 15% poison covers most of the arena, stay in safe area

[CM] MAMA

Kill Assault Knights Dodge AoE Attacks 25% Add Spawn

Assault knights spawn before fight casting near and far AoE attacks you should dodge - can /gg after killing them to buff before boss. At 25% becomes invulnerable and spawns another assault knight to kill

[CM] Capture points

Clear Adds First

More adds - clear them first, then cap

[CM] Siax

Split NSEW 66% & 33% Adds Mate Seu Add

More adds spawn. 66% adds spawn at NSEW and need to be killed, common to mark minimap of which add you will kill. 33% same adds spawn but have shifted clockwise slightly, make sure to still grab your add

[CM] Ensolyss

Dodge Inward ? Outward Slam Attack

Boss will now also do a slam attack that you should dodge inward on to avoid the smash and resulting shockwave, then outward to avoid the followup hallucination attacks
