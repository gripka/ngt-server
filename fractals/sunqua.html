Sunqua Peak

Detalhes e Mecânicas

Visualizando: Mecânicas Normais

Entrance

Use special action key Charged Leap to navigate up the mountain

Mini-Boss - Voice of the Mountain

Dodge AoEs, at 66% and 33% collect 3 orbs and use Charged Leap on the up arrow circles to destroy clouds

Waterfalls

Wait for flow of water to slow to move forward

Water Elemental

Destroy boulders to free the elemental. Occasionally a dark orb tethers to a player, stand between them to take over so no player gets 10 stacks

Mini-Boss - Fury of the Mountain

DPS boss, when meteorites drop position yourself to avoid impact and jump over their shockwaves; 66% and lower meteorites follow players briefly before dropping

Final Boss - Sorrowful Spellcaster

Fase Ar - 100%-66% - Esquive e espalhe-se para AoEs, CC barra rapidamente para parar seus ataques Quando boss fica invulnerável e invoca nuvens, use Charged Leap para destruí-las

66% Fire phase

Boss invoca 8 meteoritos para iniciar esta fase, esquive-os e fique atrás de um dos 4 finais a cair para se proteger da explosão AoE

33% Water phase

Orbes escuros spawnam, certifique-se de compartilhar conexão, jogadores também são alvos de AoEs verdes que devem compartilhar

Water split phase

Boss sobe ao ar e você é empurrado para as bordas - precisa caminhar com seu time de volta ao centro, compartilhando círculos verdes

[CM] Ai, Keeper of the Peak

You will now go straight to the final fight - Ai, Keeper of the Peak, where you will kill her twice in a Light and Dark phase

[CM] Light Phase - 100%-1%

Same phases as normal mode, just with more frequency and urgency. Note only two safe final meteorites spawn at 66%

[CM] Dark Phase - 100%-1%

You can safely /gg after killing Light Phase as there is a checkpoint. Ai's attacks now apply Fear and Confusion - condi cleanse is crucial

[CM] Fear Phase - 100%-66%

Dodge AoEs and damage the boss. If not at phased by two minutes, Fear enemies spawn and must be kited while DPSing

[CM] 66% Sorrow Phase

Boss will spawn adds that needs to be focus CCd, Fears continue to spawn

[CM] 33% Guilt Phase

A Guilt enemy tethers to a player and the player must be shared, ensure to not let it tether to boss or she buffs, multiple Sorrows spawn at once now
