Lonely Tower

Detalhes e Mecânicas

Visualizando: Mecânicas Normais

First few rooms

Solve orb puzzles to cleanse Astral nodes while other players fending off spawning enemies

Duo fight - Cerus & Deimos

Try to keep them apart (strong groups can stack and cleave them together) and avoid stepping in red ground AoE (oils), quickly kill clones that spawn

Final Boss - Eparch

Main mechanic is collecting orbs to prevent him from self-buffing during breakbar phase, prioritize these - either have an orb collector or collect orbs appropriate for your build - listed below

Red (Rage), Green (Envy), Blue (Despair)

Red (Rage): Spawned by Shockwave attack. If picked up, player +Damage dealt, +Damage taken. If Eparch absorbs, he hits harder. Green (Envy): Arrow wave attack. +Condi & boon duration. Eparch corrupts boons. These are the most important to pick up as boon corrupt can wipe runs! Blue (Despair): Fountain attack. -Damage dealt/taken. Eparch takes less damage. Ideally a support takes these

Purple (Malice) & Orange (Gluttony)

Purple (Malice): Shard target AoE. +CC power, adds self-conds. Eparch inflicts extra condis. Orange (Gluttony): Vacuum pull. Heals you initially but hurts over time. Eparch heals himself

Avoid Mechanics

Jump over shockwaves, run out when boss vacuums, dodge through arrow wave, dodge fountain projectiles, If targeted by red circle shards keep away from group

Breakbar Phase

Quickly CC breakbar when it shows up, during it Eparch absorbs orbs on ground and orb buffs players have picked up

[CM] First few rooms

Orb puzzles are harder in CM, so an advanced tactic (bug?) for the orb puzzles is to dodge backward + press interact quickly - prevents the adds from spawning. Alternatively use stealth or stability

[CM] Duo fight

Closest player gets fixated, essential to keep them separated as they get a buff together

[CM] Final Boss - Eparch

Picking up your correct color orb is now extremely critical since the stacks no longer fall off. Make sure you CC his breakbar quickly to keep your buffs high. Possibly swap rage grabber at 20 stacks since the damage taken can get intense. 66% and 33%: Champion adds spawn from two rifts, split focus them down
